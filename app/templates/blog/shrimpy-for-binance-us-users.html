What is the difference between Binance.com and Binance.us?

Binance clients based in the US have a special Binance platform that is hosted on Binance.us. If you're a US citizen, you must register on Binance.us. Binance.com is reserved for non US citizens.

That app is very similar and is fine for pretty much any usage. It complies with the US regulation and must be used when you are a US citizen. There is no point of tricking it. Some diffences are that Binance.com include Futures and Leverage tradings. that are not authorized on Binance.us.

How to follow best Shrimpy leaders with a Binance.us account?

You should already have a paid account on Shrimpy and register an account on Cryptogram. Cryptogram will need an API key to connect to your Shrimpy account.

Create a Shrimpy API key

On the Shrimpy website, go to Settings and create a new public and private API key pair. Visit your account on Cryptogram and insert these API keys in your Shrimpy account.

Note that these keys are secured by design. They only permit to rebalance your coins and not to deposit nor withdraw. For security reasons you should never provide keys that permit withdrawal to an untrusted third party application.

Once your API keys are stored you will be able to view your Shrimpy account on Cryptogram. You should then credit your account in order to buy some dynos.

Follow any Shrimpy leader from Binance.us

If you followed the previous step carefully you should have an account connected to Shrimpy with credit ready for dynos.

Dynos are processors that you rent and that follow a Shrimpy leader. When a leader has made some new research and buys or sells some cryptos, he will update his portfolio. your portfolio will be rebalanced accordingly. In the near future dynos will also be able to skim your profits into a stable coin and secure them. In other words it will take profit automatically once it reaches a level that you determine.
